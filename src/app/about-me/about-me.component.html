About Us

At Mikado Media Works, we bring stories to life with striking visuals and smart design. Based in Mumbai, we’re a full-service production house creating everything from TV commercials and animation to branded content, promos, corporate films, and digital assets.

With a strong focus on creativity and execution, we’ve delivered content across formats — long and short, vertical and widescreen — for some of India’s leading brands like Zee5, Hungama OTT, Asianet News, Big Bazaar, GroupM and Publicis Media.

Whatever the screen, whatever the story — we make it memorable.
